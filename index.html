Rivers State, Nigeria

Port Harcourt is a bustling city located in the Niger Delta region of Nigeria. Known as the "Garden City" due to its numerous parks and green spaces, it is the capital of Rivers State and a major hub for Nigeria's oil and gas industry. The city boasts a diverse cultural scene, vibrant markets, and a lively nightlife. It's home to the University of Port Harcourt, several international companies, and a busy port, which plays a vital role in the nation's economy. Despite its industrial activities, Port Harcourt maintains a blend of urban development and natural beauty, making it a unique and dynamic place to live and visit.
