History

WaveRush Adventures was founded in 2000 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who recognized the need for a premier whitewater rafting experience. Starting with just a few rafts and a dedication to adventure, the company quickly grew, gaining a reputation for safety, excitement, and exceptional service.

Over the years, WaveRush Adventures has expanded its offerings and honed its expertise, becoming a trusted name in the rafting community. Our experienced guides and top-notch equipment have helped thousands of adventurers create unforgettable memories on the water. Today, we remain committed to our founding principles of excellence, adventure, and a deep respect for nature.
